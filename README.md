## Overview

TrustMedAI is an ongoing research initiative focused on advancing trustworthy artificial intelligence for healthcare.

The project investigates semantic technologies, biomedical knowledge representation, and intelligent clinical decision support methods to improve medication safety and support healthcare professionals in evidence-informed decision-making.

This repository serves as a public overview of the project. The implementation remains private while research activities and scientific publications are in progress.

---

## Research Vision

Develop next-generation AI technologies that enhance medication safety through transparent, knowledge-driven, and clinically meaningful decision support.
